Ryzen 7 3700X

The Ryzen 7 3700X processor has been a steadfast component, performing seamlessly since its installation. Despite not fully utilizing its potential due to a bottleneck caused by my GPU, it remains a reliable and durable CPU. Its consistent performance and stability merit a solid rating of 9/10.

Lenovo Legion 5 Gaming Laptop

My Lenovo Legion 5 gaming laptop has been an invaluable asset, especially when on the move. Acquired as a Christmas gift before college, it has been reliable despite an incident that caused a cracked screen, a fault of my own. Its three-hour battery life remains adequate for my classes, and its gaming capability has been exceptional, earning a rating of 9/10.
