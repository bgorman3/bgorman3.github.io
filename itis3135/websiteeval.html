Good Website

svz.io

SVZ is a company that does webflow development, as you can tell. They have one of the best-designed websites I've seen. Â 

  • Contrast: Every single color they use is simple: black and white, black, yellow, and green. Black goes well with any color on the webpage; there isn't a single color that doesn't fit.
  • Repetition: Every element and font are the same, and it fits well at any resolution, whether it's on mobile or scrunched. The website fits so well.
  • Alignment: The structure of the page is arguably its strongest point. Everything is in a grid, and your eyes slowly follow the page down, reading box by box.
  • Proximity: The elements that are related are always right next to each other, and it's super easy to read because everything fits.

This website follows every crap element perfectly, and you can see why they work in web development. It's hard to be a web development company with a poor-looking website.

Crappy Website

zara.com

This website is hard to look at. It's a clothing website for fashion designers, but everything feels very half-hazardously placed and out of line. There are many different types of clothing for men, women, and kids, but it's hard to click on because it keeps jumping around.

  • Contrast: The contrast is decent with the white menus and dark photos, but the text stands out.
  • Repetition: This entire website has no repetition; I feel as if there are just random fonts, colors, and pictures everywhere; nothing is organized.
  • Alignment: The alignment is weird; there is a nav bar, but it's on the left, and the search bar is just randomly placed; it doesn't fit overall; there is no alignment for elements.
  • Proximity: There is a large amount of empty space everywhere on this page, and it doesn't feel well placed.

This website does not follow CRAP principles. I can see that they tried to be different and have a stylized page, but overall it just looks bad.
