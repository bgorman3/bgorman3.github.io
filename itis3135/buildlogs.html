My "First" PC

The first family PC we had was a Lenovo ThinkCentre M81. It had an SFF form factor, Intel Core i5-2400 @ 3.10 GHz, 8GB DDR3 RAM, NEW 500GB SSD, DVD-RW, Wi-Fi, VGA to HDMI Adapter, and a NEW Keyboard + Mouse. It operated without an OS.

Overall, it was a great first PC. It supported multiple monitors and could handle running some games. Unfortunately, I don't have any pictures I took myself as we disposed of this PC years ago.

My Current PC

Upon checking my motherboard's BIOS, I realized my PC is now 4 years old. It seems like it's time for an upgrade as my RX580 struggles to keep up with current AAA games. I received the upgraded PC as a Christmas gift and it has been a reliable companion since then.

Overall, my PC holds a special place for me, and I don't plan on replacing it unless necessary (or until I find a compelling reason to do so, lol). Personally, I consider this PC build as my favorite among all the ones I've assembled in the past.
